Chapter 19:
Setbacks & Slips

Healing is rarely a straight line. It’s entirely normal to experience a setback after a period of calm, successful flights. A sudden bout of turbulence or an unexpected delay can trigger old anxieties, but this does not mean you are back at square one.

Progress is non-linear. What matters most is how you respond in the aftermath. Don't let one difficult journey erase the profound progress you've made. Instead, utilize the reset protocol.

restart_alt Reset Protocol

1

Acknowledge Without Judgment

Accept that the flight was difficult without criticizing yourself for feeling anxious.

2

Re-calibrate Grounding

Practice your 5-4-3-2-1 technique in a safe space to regain emotional equilibrium.

3

Set a Micro-Goal

Define one small, achievable intention for your next flight to build back confidence.
