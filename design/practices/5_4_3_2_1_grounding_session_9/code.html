Grounding Exercise

The 5-4-3-2-1 technique

The 5-4-3-2-1 mindfulness technique grounds you in the present by naming 5 things you see, 4 things you feel, 3 things you hear, 2 things you smell and 1 thing you taste. It's a simple, sensory-based method to reduce anxiety and refocus your mind.

5

See

Acknowledge five things you see around you.

4

Feel

Acknowledge four things you can feel.

3

Hear

Acknowledge three things you can hear.

2

Smell

Acknowledge two things you can smell.

1

Taste

Acknowledge one thing you can taste.
