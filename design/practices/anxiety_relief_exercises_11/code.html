Box Breathing

Box breathing is a calming technique that involves inhaling, holding, exhaling, and holding again - each for 4 seconds. Repeating this cycle helps reduce stress, improve focus, and regulate the nervous system.

integration_instructions

How-to Guide

"Inhale to the count of 4, hold your breath for another slow count of 4, exhale through your mouth for the same slow count of 4 and then hold your breath again to the count of 4."
