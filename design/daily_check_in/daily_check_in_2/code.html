What is the Stress Compass?

The Stress Compass helps you understand which areas of life are drawing the most energy from you right now. It is not about labelling you - it is about giving you insight into your nervous system patterns.

How it works

You will rate 10 core stressor categories on a scale of 1-10. 1 is "draining me" and 10 "feels balanced". Your results create a "stress signature map" showing which nervous system pathways need support. You will receive personalized practices and micro challenges based on your unique pattern.
